## 🎯 HOW TO USE

### Adding Knowledge:
1. Document your insight/success/failure
2. Go to your SBU folder
3. Add to appropriate section
4. Submit PR to main repo
5. AI validates and stores

### Finding Knowledge:
1. Go to AI Knowledge Center
2. Search by keyword or category
3. Filter by SBU or date
4. Apply to your SBU
