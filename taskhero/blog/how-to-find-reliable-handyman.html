7 Proven Steps to Find a Reliable Handyman

1. Start with Clear Requirements

Before searching, know exactly what you need:

  • List specific tasks or projects
  • Note any special requirements (licenses, insurance)
  • Determine your budget range
  • Set a timeline for completion

2. Use a Trusted Platform

Reputable platforms like TaskHero vet all professionals through:

  • Background checks
  • Skill verification
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Insurance verification

3. Check Reviews and References

Don't just look at star ratings—dig deeper:

  • Read at least 10 recent reviews
  • Look for patterns in feedback
  • Check how the professional responds to complaints
  • Ask for references from past clients
  • Verify their Better Business Bureau rating

    • 4. Verify Credentials

    For specialized tasks, always verify:

    • Electrical work: Licensed electrician
    • Plumbing: Licensed plumber with insurance
    • Structural work: General contractor license
    • General repairs: Proof of liability insurance

    5. Get Multiple Quotes

    Always get at least 3 written estimates that include:

    • Detailed scope of work
    • Materials costs (with specifications)
    • Labor costs
    • Timeline
    • Payment terms

    6. Ask the Right Questions

    Before hiring, ask potential handymen:

    1. How many years of experience do you have?
    2. Do you have insurance? Can I see the certificate?
    3. What warranties do you offer?
    4. Who will actually be doing the work?
    5. How do you handle unexpected issues or additional costs?

    7. Start with a Small Task

    Test your handyman with a smaller project first before committing to major work. This lets you evaluate their:

    • Communication style
    • Work quality
    • Punctuality
    • Problem-solving approach

    Red Flags to Watch Out For

    🚩 Warning signs of unreliable professionals:

    • Requests payment upfront before any work
    • Can't provide proof of insurance
    • Vague or verbal estimates only
    • Pressure tactics to decide immediately
    • Much lower quotes than competitors
    • No physical address or business license
    • Negative reviews about the same issues
